# Investfair Assignment

## The brief

- Format: video, 12 minutes total
- Team: group of 5 to 8 students (HKBU Fin 7870 derivatives course)
- Topic: a derivatives product in Asia; the group chose HSI options (HKEX) plus OTC barrier options, exactly the products taught by the simulator ([[Project Overview]])
- Deadline: 2026-06-26
- Submission: wongcy@hkbu.edu.hk

## Judging rubric and how the project hits it

| Rubric area | Coverage plan |
|---|---|
| Content: history | HSI futures (1986) and HSI options (1993) on HKEX, the 1997 Asian Financial Crisis as narrative frame, HK structured products growth after 2002 |
| Content: relevance | HSI options as Asia's flagship index derivatives; barrier options dominant in HK retail structured products |
| Content: market data | HKEX derivatives volumes, contract specs (HK$50 per point), the vol levels used in the game (16% to 32%, see [[Theoretical Price Anchors]]) |
| Presentation: innovation | The booth IS a playable web game at https://zeref007.github.io/trader-simulator/; the video is a KOL-style walkthrough of it |
| Presentation: design and flow | One continuous rookie-trader story in 5 standalone clips with captions and payoff graphics ([[Payoff Diagrams]]) |
| Application: theory | [[CRR Binomial Model]] pricing (u, d, p, discounting), knock-out logic ([[buildBarrierBinomialToolTree]]), quote bands ([[Scoring System]]) |
| Application: case study | The three [[Day 4 Graduation Round]] clients with real numbers (297, 981, 1184) |
| Application: correctness | All prices verified in the engine: 186, 1112, 934, 297, 981, 1184 |

## The pitch

"Don't just read about options. Walk the desk." Classmates playing investors can open the live booth themselves; the video walks them through the four-day arc ([[Game Flow]]) as a story.

## Constraints noted in the plan

No real-person impersonation: the host is "Jordan Bull", an original high-energy 1990s HK broker persona voiced by TTS, not a clone of any actor. Production details in [[Video Pipeline]]; the clip-by-clip plan in [[Script]].
